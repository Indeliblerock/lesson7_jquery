Spaghetti and Meatsauce

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans diced tomatos
  • 4 chopped mushrooms
  • 1 (10 ounce) bag of spinach
  • 2 teaspoons basil
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 packet dried spaghetti noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

Combine ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large saucepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. Drain grease.

Add tomatos, basil, mushrooms, spinach, 1 teaspoon salt, and pepper

Simmer until sauce is thick

Bring a large pot of water to a boil

Add olive oil, dried spaghetti noodles, and 1 teaspoon salt to the pot, stir until noodles are tender

Separate water from noodles, drain water

Combine noodles and sauce
